This site is a resource for individuals and communities in Portland seeking to heal from, respond to and prevent hateful acts.

Powered by Portland United Against Hate (PUAH), a coalition of community-based organizations, neighborhood associations, concerned communities, and the City of Portland.

Witness or experience a hate incident?

Report it online or get connected with a PUAH partner and report in person. Tell us only what you want to, it's all confidential.
