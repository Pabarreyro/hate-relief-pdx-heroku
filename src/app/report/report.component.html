Reporting Options

Online

Anonymously

You agree to let Portland United Against Hate use the information you submit according to the terms of their privacy policy, but you will not be asked to provide information that could personally identify you.

Nonanonymously

You agree to let Portland United Against Hate use the information you submit according to the terms of their privacy policy, and would like to be contacted by a PUAH partner regarding your experience.

In Person

Find a PUAH Partner Match

You agree to let Portland United Against Hate use personal information to connect you with one of our member organizations so you can report in person.
